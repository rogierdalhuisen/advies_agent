# Global Prima Medical Insurance (GPMI)

Global Prima Medical Insurance offers flexibility with five plan options, various coverage regions, and multiple deductible and currency payment choices. The program provides different underwriting approaches to help create coverage that fits individual needs, considering medical history. IMG is committed to managing healthcare costs to keep insurance affordable.

## 1. Global Prima Medical Insurance

Global Prima Medical Insurance provides annually renewable coverage for individuals living or working outside their home country, typically for one year or longer. It offers 24/7 global access to healthcare and international medical services, including coordination of emergency evacuations.

GPMI plans can be customized for:
*   Expats & Their Families
*   Digital Nomads
*   Group Coverage
*   Dual Residences
*   Multinational Employers
*   Local Nationals

## 2. Coverage, Benefits & Plan Highlights

**Core Benefits:**
*   In-patient and Out-patient care and treatment: Coverage for hospital stays, surgeries, and related treatments.
*   Diagnostic Tests: Laboratory tests, CT, PET, and MRI.
*   Prescription Drugs: Coverage for prescribed medications.
*   Cancer Treatment: Comprehensive oncology care.
*   Vaccinations: Immunizations as per medical recommendations.
*   Rehabilitation and Nursing at Home: Post-hospitalization care and support.
*   Well-Being: Coverage for Hearing tests and Routine health checks.
*   Crisis Benefits: Evacuation and repatriation.

**Health Services Included:**
*   Telemedicine Services: 24/7 online and telephone access to medical professionals for new, non-emergency medical issues.
*   Support Programme: Telemedicine for mental health, supporting financial, physical, and emotional well-being.
*   Travel Intelligence: Provides dynamic alerts and country intelligence to help prepare for and stay safe while abroad 24/7. Expats receive location-specific push notifications spanning 10 threat categories (crime, terrorism, severe weather, etc.).

## 3. Plan Options

**Choosing the right plan:**
Global Prima Medical Insurance offers several plan options, areas of coverage, multiple deductibles, and modes of payment. Different underwriting methods are available to extend medical coverage, even for those who might be declined by other companies.

Global Prima Medical Insurance offers five levels of coverage:
*   **Bronze:** Annual Maximum Benefit: €1,000,000
*   **Bronze Plus:** Annual Maximum Benefit: €2,000,000
*   **Silver:** Annual Maximum Benefit: €3,000,000
*   **Gold:** Annual Maximum Benefit: €4,000,000
*   **Platinum:** Annual Maximum Benefit: €5,000,000

**Optional Add-ons:**
*   **Routine Pregnancy (10-month waiting period):** Covers routine pregnancy and childbirth costs, including pre and postnatal check-ups (maximum 12), scans (maximum three, one per trimester), and delivery costs for a routine delivery, if this option is added to your plan.
*   **Dental Treatments (6-month waiting period):** A full range of dental procedures can be included in the plan, in addition to emergency dental coverage.

*Please check the policy documents to confirm which benefits apply to your chosen plan.*

## 4. Premium & Quote Information

Premiums vary based on factors such as age, chosen plan, optional add-ons, and deductible levels.

**Age limits:**
*   Adults up to 75 years of age at the time of application. Higher ages may be accepted subject to medical underwriting.
*   Once enrolled and coverage accepted, renewal is offered indefinitely, provided continuous coverage is maintained.
*   Children can be insured as dependents from birth up to 25 years of age. Child-alone policies are available, subject to underwriting approval.

**Restricted countries:**
IMG operates with a list of sanctioned countries for which coverage cannot be provided. Potential customers should also verify the health insurance requirements of their planned destination countries to ensure their chosen cover complies with local mandates.

## 5. Claims Process

**Key Highlights:**
*   **24/7 Global Support:** Multilingual advisors are available around the clock via phone, email, chat, Messenger, and WhatsApp.
*   **MyIMG App:** Available for paperless claims for plans that cover from the first euro/dollar.
*   **Direct Billing:** Hospital charges can be handled directly 24/7, eliminating out-of-pocket payments during medical emergencies.
*   **Dedicated Team:** A multilingual claims management team operates from Cardif UK.
*   **Claim Reimbursement:** Freedom to choose your hospital network or doctors.
*   **Partner Network:** IMG maintains a medical network worldwide and in the USA (First Health and United Health Care).

For improved user experience, communication, and efficiency, it is recommended to submit claims via the MyIMG member portal. A full range of downloadable forms is available on the IMG website. For more details about the GPMI claims process, a step-by-step guide can be downloaded from their website.
