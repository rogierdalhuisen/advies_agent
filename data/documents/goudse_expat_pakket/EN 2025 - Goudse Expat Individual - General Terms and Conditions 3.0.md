**When can you appael to the insurances you took out?**
If you have damages you can only appeal to the insurances in this package if the requirement of uncertainty is met, as referred to in article 7:925 of the Civil Code (Burgelijk Wetboek).

If there is doubt we must be able to demonstrate that you knew or could have foreseen that the event was expected. Is an event not covered? Then legal disputes which follow from this event or which are connected therewith are also not covered.

#### 2.1.2 What is not insured?

We do not reimburse loss or damage resulting from:

- **committing or participating in a crime;**
- **by the possession or use of weapons by an insured;**
- **intent or recklessness of an insured;**
- **a row, fight or dangerous action**
  - Exception: if the row, fight or dangerous action was necessary. Because it was a lawful manner to defend oneself. Or because it was an attempt to rescue oneself or another person or persons.
- **as a result of being under the influence of alcohol**
  - Being under the influence of alcohol is at any rate involved if the blood alcohol content is 0.8 promille or more or if the breath alcohol content is 350 micrograms or more.
- **the use of or the addiction to drugs**
  - Drugs are intoxicating, narcotic, stimulating or similar substances. Exception: the insured person uses drugs after consultation with a physician. The insured person must however follow the instructions of the physician. He must also follow the instructions of use.
- **dangerous sports**
- **nuclear reactions**
  - Not covered is the loss or damage caused by, occurring during or arising from nuclear reactions irrespective of how the reaction originated. This exclusion does not apply to medical treatment whereby radioactive radiation was used during treatment.

#### 2.1.3 What is covered in the event of acts of war of wilful damage, terrorism or malicious contamination?

Is the insured person victim to an act of war or warlike situation and/or terrorism and/or malicious contamination? And there was no active participation to this act of war or warlike situation and/or terrorism?

Then the insured is covered for SOS-assistance, Medical Expenses insurance and the Dental Expenses insurance. Provided the costs fall within the applicable limits.

For the Accidents insurance and Occupational Disability insurance:

- No coverage when the insured is traveling to or from, or stay in a country or territory which is well known that there is a situation of an act of war or warlike situation;
- Cover during the first 15 days in the event of the sudden and unforeseen coming about of an act of war or warlike situation. After these 15 days, the cover will automatically lapse. During these 15 days there is no cover for malicious contamination.

Then there is no insurance coverage for Travel Luggage and Cancellation, Legal, Contents and Private Liability.

If it is stated above that there is no cover, your damage can sometimes be presented to the NHT. You can read what that means below.

**Important damage from terrorism**
Due to the attack in New York on September 11, 2001 it has become clear that terrorism can have much larger and more drastic consequences than was assumed until then. Insurers in the Netherlands therefore, founded the Dutch Terrorism Risk Reinsurance Company (NHT). This is used bring under the risks of terrorisme.

**NHT makes binding ruling**
Do you have damages associated with terrorism, tell us as soon as possible. We claim your damage with the NHT. The NHT determines whether the event falls under the terrorism risk. The judgment of the NHT is binding for you and and us.

**Your coverage**
Is the damage caused by terrorism in a calendar year for all participating insurers to the NHT together exceed one billion? Then the NHT determines a payment percentage. The cover is in that case limited to the amount that the NHT pays us.

The total damage remains under one billion, or does the NHT not pay us for some other reason? In that case we pay out as is stated in your policy conditions.

**Loss of cover**
You can report your damage with us until two years after the NHT has made its ruling. Then Your coverage lapses.

**More information?**
The precise definition of the term terrorism risk can be found in the 'Clauses Sheet terrorism cover with the NHT'. There you can read exactly how the limitation of coverage works. The full text of this clauses sheet and the "Claims Settlement Protocol" can be found at: www.terrorismeverzekerd.nl

#### 2.1.4 When do we speak of an act of war or warlike situation?

This situation is at any rate involved if the Ministry of Foreign Affairs has issued a travel advice that is qualified as: All travel is advised against/All travel to certain areas is advised against.

This travel advice will be published via: http://www.minbuza.nl/reizen-en-landen/reisadviezen. With regard to the Accidents insurance and the Occupational Disability insurance malicious contamination by radioactive substances will be excluded under all circumstances.

#### 2.1.5 (Sanction) legislation and regulations

We do not provide cover and do not pay out if this is not allowed by the national or international (sanction) laws and regulations. We also do not provide cover and do not reimburse damage if there is prohibited trade in strategic or embargo goods or services.
