# Globe Traveller Insurance

## 1. Introduction

The Globe Traveller Insurance is a single-trip worldwide travel insurance provided by the French insurance broker ACS. This comprehensive online insurance offers "first euro" coverage for emergency medical health and repatriation, along with coverage for accidents, liability, assistance, luggage, and travel delays. Three optional covers are also available.

## 2. Important Information

*   Suitable for any nationality.
*   Available up to the age of 65 years.
*   For trips outside the usual country of residence.
*   Minimum trip duration: 1 day; maximum: 12 months.
*   Optional Risk Sports option.
*   Optional Sport Equipment option.
*   Policies can be issued at any moment. However, if you have already departed from your country of residence, an 8-day waiting period applies before coverage begins.

### Risk Sports Covered (with optional add-on):
Bobsleigh, skeleton, mountaineering, competitive luge, aerial sports (with the exception of parasailing), sea scooters, combat sports, off-piste sliding sports, scuba diving with tanks.

*   The policy can be issued online.
*   Premium payment is via credit card.
*   It is strongly recommended to read the policy conditions to understand what is covered and what is not in detail.

## 5. Traveling Through Multiple Zones of Cover

When applying online, you must select the zone(s) of cover for your trip (Europe, Russia*, Africa, North America, Latin America, Middle East and Western Asia, Asia and Oceania). If you plan to visit multiple zones, you must select all intended zones.

*Russia is currently excluded from cover.

## 6. Modification or Cancellation of the Policy

Any modification of dates or cancellation of the contract is only accepted if:

1.  It was notified before the effective date of the contract, and;
2.  Before the Member’s departure from their usual country of residence, and;
3.  Duly justified (e.g., refusal of a travel visa).

Changes of dates are possible only once during the current calendar year.
For cancellations, the policy will only be reimbursed upon presentation of supporting documents (e.g., visa refusal), less a €20 fee.
The policy is agreed for a fixed period with no automatic renewal. It may not be canceled or reimbursed during the insured period.

All requests for cancellation/modification can be sent to serviceclient@acs-ami.com. Please write in English and always include your policy number.
