# Cigna Close Care

## 1. Introduction

Cigna Close Care is the budget version of Cigna's international health insurance, offered alongside their comprehensive Cigna Global Health Options (CGHO) health insurance.

### Key Features:
*   Coverage in the country of residence and in the country of origin (home country - maximum 180 days per year).
*   Maximum Annual Overall Benefit: €400,000.
*   Maximum Annual Benefit per Condition: €200,000.
*   Coverage options available for: medical care, outpatient, dental.
*   No coverage for: international medical evacuation, vision, maternity.
*   Reimbursement only for ambulance transport, not for ambulance planes/helicopters. This makes the insurance less suitable for remote areas without a good hospital nearby.
*   Early termination is not possible if a claim has been submitted in the current insurance year.

### Coverage During Travel (Out of Area Emergency Cover):
*   Coverage during travel refers to "temporary trips outside your country of habitual residence or country of nationality."
*   Maximum reimbursement: €29,600.
*   Coverage for a maximum of 21 consecutive travel days and a maximum of 45 travel days per year.
*   Coverage for unforeseen and urgent medical emergencies (inpatient, day-patient).
*   Urgent outpatient costs are only covered if the outpatient module is included, up to a maximum of €1,850.

### Countries Where Coverage is Not Possible
Cigna may not offer insurance for certain countries. This will be indicated in the online module if applicable to your country of residence.

### Coverage Options
1.  **Core Module:** Medical care (inpatient and day-patient).
2.  **Optional:** Outpatient.
3.  **Optional:** Dental.
4.  **No Cover for:** Vision, Maternity.
