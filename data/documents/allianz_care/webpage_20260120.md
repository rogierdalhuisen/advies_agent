# Allianz Care - International Health Insurance

Allianz Care International Health Insurance is a comprehensive international health insurance available for companies and individuals abroad.

This insurance covers costs such as hospitalizations, diagnostic tests, post-hospital treatment, psychotherapy, and psychiatry. Allianz Care also provides coverage for pregnancy and childbirth. The Core plan, even with the lowest coverage, offers reimbursement for a single room hospital stay. Outpatient and Dental care can be optionally added.

## 1. Introduction

Allianz Worldwide Care's International Healthcare Insurance is an international health insurance available for companies (Collective) and individuals (Individual), including family members. Information regarding insurance for individuals and families is provided below.

Key features:
*   Available for EU nationalities (a co-insured partner may also have a non-EU nationality).
*   Enrollment up to age 75.
*   **10% discount on premiums for JoHo Insurances clients.**
*   Once accepted, the insurance can continue for life (automatic yearly renewal), if desired.
*   Mid-term cancellation (with premium refund) during an ongoing insurance year due to return to the home country is possible, requiring proof (boarding pass / utility bill).
*   "Locals" can also be co-insured.
*   War and civil unrest coverage (Molestdekking) applies.
*   Allianz Worldwide Care is based in Ireland and should not be confused with "local" Allianz insurers, such as Allianz Netherlands.

## 2. Coverage

### Key Points:
*   The Core Plan offers a deductible ranging from 0 to 6,000 euros per year.
*   The same medical coverage must be chosen for everyone on the same policy.
*   Coverage for pregnancy and childbirth is included in Core Plan Enhanced or Signature.
*   The policy uses the terms "inpatient" and "outpatient" costs.

### Coverage Overview:
*   The insurance has 3 coverage levels: Base, Enhanced, and Signature. These levels apply to the Core plan (inpatient coverage), Outpatient plan, and Dental plan.
*   Choosing a Core plan is mandatory.
*   The insurance covers costs such as hospitalizations, diagnostic tests, post-hospital treatment, psychotherapy and psychiatry, and various forms of specialist care.
*   Core plan Enhanced and Signature also include coverage for pregnancy and childbirth.
*   Separate modules are available for Outpatient coverage and Dental care.
*   Repatriation is included in the Core plan.
*   For a detailed overview, refer to the Table of Benefits.

### Explanation of Coverage Choices:
*   **Country of residence**: The country where you will reside.
*   **Nationality**: The nationality of the applicant and primary insured.
*   **Payment currency**: The currency in which you wish to pay the premium; coverage limits are also related to this currency.
*   **Geographical area of cover**:
    *   If full coverage in the USA is desired, you must choose "Worldwide".
    *   If you do not live in the USA and choose "Excluding USA", you will have coverage in the USA for unforeseen emergency medical matters during holidays and travel, for a continuous period of 6 weeks.
    *   If you live in Africa, you have the option for "Cover Africa Only". This is more affordable but offers no or limited coverage outside Africa, meaning treatments and operations cannot be planned or undergone in other countries (e.g., Netherlands).
*   Within the chosen **Geographical area of cover**, you are free to choose where you receive treatment. You are not limited to care in your country of residence. You can plan and undergo treatments in another country, as long as that country is part of your Geographical area of cover. Transportation and accommodation costs are at your own expense.
*   **Payment method and frequency**: Various payment methods and frequencies are available. A surcharge applies for non-annual payments. The surcharge for each payment term is specified.
*   **Policy holder age**: The age of the policyholder and primary insured.
*   **Add family members**: Indicate how many family members you wish to insure, including the age of your partner and the number and ages of children.
*   **Outpatient cover required?**: Indicate yes or no.
*   **Deductible?**: Indicate if you wish to have a deductible. You can choose a deductible for both the Core plan and the Outpatient plan.
*   **Dental?**: Indicate if you also want coverage for dental costs.

## 3. Premium & Quote

You can request a quote for Allianz Health Insurance via the Allianz Care quote request form. You will receive a quote from Allianz, including the coverage overview and terms and conditions.

## 4. How to apply for the insurance?

Along with the quote, you will receive an application form. If you wish to apply for the insurance after receiving the quote, you can submit the completed application form + health declaration + copy of ID/passport. Ensure all insured persons aged 18 and over sign the form. Allianz will then process the application.

**10% discount?**
JoHo has a special temporary discount agreement with Allianz Care: JoHo Insurances clients receive a 10% discount on premiums, as long as the promotion is active.

**Countries where coverage is not possible**
Currently, individual health insurance cannot be purchased if you reside in one of the following countries:
*   Brazil
*   Dubai
*   Germany
*   Iran
*   Ireland
*   North Korea
*   Russia
*   Any country subject to sanctions

Note: In some countries, local health insurance is legally mandatory. An international private health insurance may not meet these local requirements.

## 5. Services after the insurance has started

For certain types of treatments, you need prior authorization from the insurer before the treatment takes place. If you undergo treatment without prior authorization, the insurer has the right not to reimburse the costs (fully). This does not apply to emergencies, although in such cases, contact must be made with the insurer as soon as possible. More information about the Treatment Guarantee can be found in the Benefit Guide.

### Submitting Claims
*   Claims can be easily submitted using the claim form.
*   You can submit claims to Allianz using the MyHealth app or the online portal.
