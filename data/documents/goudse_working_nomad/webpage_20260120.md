# Goudse Working Nomad Travel Insurance

The Working Nomad insurance is designed for Dutch and Belgian citizens who reside abroad for a minimum of 6 months and a maximum of 10 years for living, working, or traveling. This insurance is suitable for digital nomads, backpackers, volunteers, people in a bridging period during emigration, or those with temporary paid work.

Coverage includes emergency assistance (SOS), unexpected medical expenses, repatriation, medical evacuation, and return due to family emergencies. Optional additional coverage for luggage, accidents, and liability is available.

## 1. Target Audience

This travel insurance is intended for Dutch citizens (under 55 years old) who will be staying abroad for at least 6 months for living, working, and/or traveling.

- Travelers
- Backpackers
- Digital Nomads
- Working Nomads
- Volunteers
- Bridging period during emigration
- Working abroad

This insurance focuses on long-term stays (maximum duration of 10 years) abroad and is aimed at individuals who want to be insured for serious incidents during an extended period abroad. This includes, for example, hospitalization due to a serious illness or accident. In such situations, the insurer may decide on medical evacuation and definitive return to the Netherlands.

This travel insurance is **not suitable** for people who:

- Have emigrated and no longer plan to return to the Netherlands. This insurance has a maximum duration and an end age.
- Desire more extensive coverage, for example, for chronic conditions, pregnancy, childbirth, or non-urgent care.
- Perform dangerous activities or work.
- Wish to stay in or travel to high-risk areas.

## 2. Features, Considerations & Limitations

### Key Features:

- **Flexible Coverage:** Standard SOS and medical expenses insurance with optional extensions for luggage & household contents, liability, and accidents.
- **Flexible Region Coverage:** Worldwide coverage including or excluding the USA.
- **Long-term Stay:** Suitable for stays from 6 months up to a maximum of 10 years, ideal for living, working, or traveling abroad.
- **Age Limits:** Maximum age of 45 for worldwide coverage including the USA, and 55 for worldwide coverage excluding the USA.
- **Repatriation:** Under certain conditions, reimbursement of travel costs for return to the Netherlands due to medical repatriation or treatment in the Netherlands under the Dutch basic health insurance.
- **Flexible Payment:** Monthly premium payment via direct debit from an EU SEPA account.
- **Suitable for Deregistered Individuals:** Also suitable for people who have deregistered from their Dutch municipality and no longer have basic health insurance.
- **Netherlands:** Coverage during a temporary visit to the Netherlands.

### Considerations:

- **Exclusions:** Certain professions and dangerous sports are excluded from coverage. Please read the policy conditions carefully for details.
- **End Age:** Note the maximum end age, depending on the chosen coverage.
- **Repatriation:** Reimbursement for repatriation is subject to conditions. Check the policy conditions for exact terms.
- **Premium:** The premium may vary depending on the chosen coverage, age, and destination. The premium is charged per full month.
- **Waiting Period:** If you are already abroad when you take out the insurance, a waiting period of 3 days applies. Your insurance coverage starts on the third day after the effective date. If the insurance starts on June 1st, you have coverage from June 4th at 00:00. This waiting period does not apply in case of an accident.

### Limitations:

The travel insurance offers no or very limited coverage for:

- Individuals aged 55 and older. In the USA, individuals aged 45 and older.
- Pregnancy and childbirth.
- Physiotherapy and psychiatric care.
- Medical costs related to or resulting from pre-existing conditions.
- Preventive care.
- Non-urgent care.
- Emigrants, permanent residence abroad.
- Dangerous activities and sports.
- Stays in high-risk countries and areas.

### USA:

For long-term visits to the USA, choose "Worldwide including USA" region coverage. If you are outside the USA and have chosen "Worldwide excluding USA" region coverage, but briefly visit the USA during your stay abroad, temporary coverage in the USA is still provided in the following situations:

- During a stopover (maximum 2 days).
- During tourist trips of a maximum of 28 days in the USA. For a planned trip of > 28 days, there is no coverage for the entire trip with "Worldwide excluding USA" region coverage.

**Consider this travel insurance if you:**

- Are a Dutch citizen planning to live, work, or travel abroad for an extended period.
- Want affordable and flexible coverage with the option to add supplementary coverages.
- Are looking for insurance that is also suitable if you have deregistered from your municipality.

**Note:** Always read the full policy conditions before purchasing insurance to ensure it meets your needs.

## 3. Coverages

### SOS Assistance (Mandatory Component)

- **Medical Evacuation:** Transport to a suitable hospital or back to the Netherlands in case of serious illness or accident.
- **Return due to Family Circumstances:** Coverage of travel costs in case of serious illness, accident, or death of a family member in the Netherlands.
- **Repatriation of Remains:** Repatriation of remains to the Netherlands.
- **Communication and Accommodation Costs:** Reimbursement of costs for communication with family/emergency services and additional accommodation costs if necessary.
- **Search, Salvage, and Rescue:** Coverage of costs for search and rescue operations in case of emergency.

### Medical Expenses (Mandatory Component)

- **Emergency Medical Care:** Coverage up to €2,000,000 per event for acute medical treatments, including hospitalization and surgeries.
- **Planned Care:** Coverage up to a maximum of €5,000 per event for pre-planned medical treatments.
- **Medical Expenses Deductible:** €75 per event, in the Netherlands €100 per event.
- **Necessary Medication:** Reimbursement of medication costs up to 180 days after a covered event.
- **Dental Care:** Coverage up to €500 for dental treatments after an accident.

### Optional Coverages

In addition to the mandatory SOS and medical expenses insurance, you can choose the following optional supplementary coverages:

### Luggage & Household Contents

- Maximum reimbursement of €4,000 per insurance year for damage to or loss of luggage and/or household contents.
- One-time reimbursement of €300 for damage to or loss of your phone during the entire duration of the insurance.

### Personal Liability

- Coverage up to €1,000,000 per event for damage you accidentally cause to others or their property.

### Accident Coverage

- Payout of €30,000 in case of death due to an accident.
- Payout up to a maximum of €100,000 in case of permanent disability due to an accident.

**Note:**

- The exact conditions and reimbursements for all coverages, including optional coverages, are stated in the Coverage Overview. Read this overview carefully before making a choice.
- The premium for the insurance varies depending on the chosen coverages and other factors.
