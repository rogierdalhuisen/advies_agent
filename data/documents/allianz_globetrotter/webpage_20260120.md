# Allianz Global Assistance - Globetrotter Travel Insurance

The Allianz Globetrotter Insurance is a comprehensive travel insurance for long-term stays outside the Netherlands. It can be taken out even if you no longer have health insurance in the Netherlands; in this case, choose the 'Medical Costs Full' option. Alternatively, you can supplement your existing health insurance with the 'Medical Costs Supplementary' option. With the Globetrotter, you can be insured for a maximum of 2 consecutive years, including one interim visit to the Netherlands for the entire period.

## 1. Globetrotter Highlights

*   **Full coverage for emergency medical care:** Also valid if you are no longer entitled to Dutch health insurance.
*   **Valid even if deregistered from the Personal Records Database:** You retain coverage even after deregistering from your municipality.
*   **Risky activities can be insured + immediate coverage:** For example, if you plan to do dangerous work, such as with heavy agricultural machinery or wild animals. A surcharge may apply.

## 2. Study, Internship, Volunteer Work, or Temporary Paid Work?

Allianz Global Assistance requires prior notification of your exact activities. A surcharge applies to certain types of work. If the surcharge is not paid, you are not insured.

If you know before departure that you will be undertaking a volunteer project or temporary job, you can declare this, and it will be determined if a surcharge is required. If you wish to keep your options open and spontaneously join a project during your trip, you must contact Allianz Global Assistance from abroad to determine if a surcharge is needed. If a surcharge is applicable, it will be charged for the entire insured period, and a 3-day waiting period applies after the policy adjustment. Be aware of this when taking out the Globetrotter to avoid being uninsured.

## 3. Globetrotter Coverage Options

There are two coverage options: Standard and Super.

### Standard Coverage

*   SOS assistance
*   Travel luggage up to a maximum of €1,500
*   Medical costs (supplementary and full)
*   Winter sports and adventurous sports

### Super Coverage

*   SOS assistance
*   Travel luggage up to a maximum of €3,500
*   Medical costs (supplementary and full)
*   Winter sports and adventurous sports
*   Legal assistance
*   Accidents
*   Liability

Incidents such as theft, hospital admission, or forced return to the Netherlands are all covered. Accidents during winter sports, underwater sports, and work activities (excluding extra hazardous activities, see conditions) are also covered with Super coverage. The full coverage overview details the scope of coverage and maximum reimbursements.
