# Special ISIS Travel Insurance

The JoHo Special ISIS insurance is designed for those going abroad for an extended period – whether for backpacking, working, studying, internships, or volunteering. The insurance offers coverage during travel and for activities on-site, and is suitable for flexible stays from a few months to several years.

## 1. Target Groups & Characteristics

### Suitable for travel with the purpose of:
*   World trip, backpacking, and gap year
*   Holiday work (e.g., hotel, camping, hostel, bar, lodge, and similar activities)
*   Internship
*   Study
*   Volunteer work

This travel insurance is specifically designed for backpackers, world travelers, and adventurous activities.

## 2. Characteristics

*   **Area**: Europe, Worldwide (including or excluding USA) coverage.
*   **Duration (Term)**: From a minimum of 2 months to a maximum of 4 years.
*   **Age**: From 16 years up to and including 54 years (All-in coverage) or 65 years (with Extra coverage).
*   **Medical Coverage**: Focused on unforeseen and emergency assistance (both emergency and outpatient costs).
*   **Insurer**: De Goudse, the most experienced insurer for young people and backpackers.
*   **Health Insurance Coverage**: Also provides coverage if your Dutch or Belgian health insurance no longer offers it. You must always choose the 'All In' coverage. The All-in coverage is also mandatory if you still have health insurance (Mutualities) in Belgium.

## 3. Coverage

### What does the Special ISIS travel insurance cover?
The insurance can offer coverage for:
*   Medical costs
*   Luggage
*   Liability
*   Accident coverage
*   Legal assistance
*   Repatriation to the Netherlands or Belgium in case of illness or death of a 1st or 2nd degree family member

### What choices can you make?
You choose your region: Europe, World, or World including USA.
Additionally, you choose a coverage level: Standard, Super, or Super+.

*   **Standard**: The lowest premium.
*   **Super**: Higher coverage and lower deductible for, among other things, luggage, medical costs, liability, and legal assistance.
*   **Super+**: The highest coverage, with no deductible for luggage, and maximum reimbursement for, among other things, mobile phones, legal assistance, and accidents.

For medical costs, there are three options:
*   **All-in**: Coverage up to cost price, even without Dutch health insurance.
*   **Extra**: Coverage up to cost price, supplementary to your Dutch health insurance.
*   **Basic**: No coverage for medical costs.
