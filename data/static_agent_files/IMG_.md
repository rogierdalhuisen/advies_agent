# Global Prima Medical Insurance (GPMI)

A comprehensive international health insurance for individuals and families living or working outside their home country, offered by IMG. Distinguished by its high flexibility — five coverage tiers, multiple deductible options, choice of coverage region, and different underwriting approaches that can accommodate people with pre-existing conditions who might be declined elsewhere.

Suited for a wide range of international profiles including expats, digital nomads, dual residents, and multinational employees. Unlike more basic expat travel products, GPMI covers chronic conditions, cancer, pregnancy (optional), dental (optional), and preventative care — making it a genuine long-term healthcare solution rather than emergency-only coverage.
