# Allianz Care International Health Insurance

A comprehensive international health insurance from Allianz Worldwide Care (Ireland), available for EU nationals and their families living abroad. Focused purely on healthcare — inpatient coverage is the mandatory core, with optional outpatient and dental modules on top.

Stands out for its high enrollment age (up to 75), lifetime renewal once accepted, and inclusion of psychotherapy and psychiatry even in the base plan. Pregnancy is covered from the Enhanced tier upward. A solid choice for individuals or families seeking a dedicated, long-term international health insurance without the broader package bundling of products like Goudse or OOM.
