# Goudse NGO/Missionary Package

A comprehensive all-in-one insurance package for Dutch and Belgian nationals living and working long-term in low-income or developing countries. Tailored for people operating outside the typical corporate expat context — such as NGO workers, missionaries, volunteers, teachers, medical workers, aid workers, and founders of development-focused foundations who work for a local salary or no salary at all.

The package bundles healthcare, liability, household contents, travel, and more into a single solution, making it suited for people committing to an extended stay (at least one year) in challenging environments where comprehensive, integrated coverage matters more than flexibility.
