# Goudse Expat Package

A comprehensive all-in-one insurance package from De Goudse, tailored specifically for Dutch and Belgian nationals living and working abroad — whether seconded, locally employed, freelance, or self-employed. Bundles healthcare, SOS assistance, dental, household contents, travel baggage, liability, legal assistance, accidents, and disability into a single Dutch-law policy.

Positioned as the more complete and mature sibling of the Goudse NGO package — aimed at a broader expat population rather than development/aid workers specifically. Notable for its family-friendly structure (single shared deductible), transferability between countries, and one of the few expat products offering ACA-compliant USA coverage.
