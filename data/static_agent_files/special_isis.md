# Special ISIS Travel Insurance

A travel insurance from De Goudse, distributed via JoHo, aimed at young people going abroad for extended adventurous or purposeful trips — backpackers, gap year travelers, interns, students, and volunteers. Covers the period from departure to return, including activities on-site.

Distinct from expat or residence-based insurance — this is a travel product for people on the move rather than settling somewhere. Flexible in duration (2 months to 4 years) and modular in coverage, with options ranging from basic emergency assistance to comprehensive all-in medical coverage for those without active Dutch or Belgian health insurance.
