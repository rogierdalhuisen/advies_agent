# Cigna Global Health Insurance

A premium international health insurance from Cigna, a major American insurer with an extensive global provider network. Available to all nationalities with no maximum age and unlimited policy duration, making it one of the more accessible products for older applicants or those seeking lifelong coverage.

Highly customizable in terms of coverage levels, deductibles, and co-payments, but comes at a higher price point than comparable products. Distinguished by its large network but notable for requiring pre-approval even for routine GP visits — more administratively demanding than most alternatives. Best suited for expats who prioritize network breadth and long-term continuity over cost efficiency.
