# OOM Living Abroad Insurance

A flexible, modular insurance package from OOM Verzekeringen for Dutch and Belgian nationals living abroad long-term or indefinitely — emigrants, expats, retirees, digital nomads, and entrepreneurs. Covers health, SOS, dental, travel baggage, liability, legal assistance, accidents, and contents, each selectable independently.

The open-ended, cancellable-daily contract structure makes it the long-term counterpart to OOM's Temporary Abroad product — designed for people who have no fixed return date rather than those on a defined temporary stay. A practical, no-frills package without the Dutch/Belgian-only branding of Goudse products, but similarly broad in scope.
