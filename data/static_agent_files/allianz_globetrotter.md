# Allianz Global Assistance Globetrotter Insurance

A long-term travel insurance from Allianz Global Assistance for Dutch nationals staying abroad for up to 2 years, including those who have deregistered from the Netherlands and no longer hold Dutch health insurance. Offers both full and supplementary medical cost options, making it flexible depending on whether existing health coverage is still active.

Geared toward adventurous and active travelers — risky activities and dangerous work can be declared and insured upfront, which distinguishes it from more restrictive travel products. A practical choice for people on extended trips who want solid travel coverage without committing to a full expat insurance package.
