# ACS Globe Traveller Insurance

A straightforward single-trip travel insurance from French broker ACS, covering emergency medical costs, repatriation, accidents, liability, luggage, and travel delays for one trip of up to 12 months. Available to any nationality up to age 65, making it one of the more accessible products in the portfolio.

Purely a trip-based product — not for long-term stays or residency abroad. Best suited for travelers who need solid emergency and medical coverage for a defined journey, with optional add-ons for risk sports and sports equipment.
