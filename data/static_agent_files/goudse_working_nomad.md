# Goudse Working Nomad Travel Insurance

A long-term travel insurance for Dutch and Belgian citizens who live, work, or travel abroad for an extended period — think digital nomads, backpackers, volunteers, and people in a transitional phase between countries. It bridges the gap between short-term travel insurance and full expat health coverage, covering serious incidents like hospitalization, medical evacuation, and emergency repatriation.

Not designed for comprehensive healthcare — chronic conditions, pregnancy, and non-urgent care fall outside its scope. Best suited for relatively young, healthy individuals (under 55) seeking affordable, flexible protection during an open-ended stay abroad rather than a permanent settlement.
