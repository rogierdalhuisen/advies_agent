# OOM Temporary Abroad Insurance

A modular insurance package from OOM Verzekeringen for Dutch and Belgian nationals living or staying abroad temporarily — up to a maximum of 2 years. Covers health, dental, travel, liability, legal assistance, accidents, and contents, with each module selectable independently.

Positioned as a flexible short-to-medium term solution: suitable as a bridge insurance, for secondments, volunteer work, or retirement abroad, rather than a permanent expat solution. Distinguished by its coverage of many adventurous and risky activities, and the ability to apply even when already abroad. The hard 2-year maximum duration is an important characteristic — it is genuinely not suited for long-term or permanent stays.
