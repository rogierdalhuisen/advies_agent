# International Expat Insurance (Henner / Allianz)

A modular international expat insurance package from Henner, operating on behalf of Allianz, designed for expats and their families living outside their home country. Open to all nationalities, with medical expenses as the core component and optional add-ons for dental, disability, life cover, and accident insurance.

Distinguished by its modularity and the inclusion of non-medical financial risks — making it more than just a health insurance. Suited for expats who want a single package covering both healthcare and income/life protection. Coverage area is worldwide, though USA residency is not permitted and USA coverage is limited to emergency stays.
