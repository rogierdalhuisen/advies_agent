# Cigna Close Care

A budget international health insurance from Cigna, designed for expats seeking affordable coverage in their country of residence and home country. Covers inpatient medical care as the core, with optional outpatient and dental add-ons.

Notably limited compared to fuller expat health products — no international medical evacuation, no maternity, no vision, and travel coverage capped at 45 days per year. Best suited for expats in countries with reliable hospital infrastructure nearby, where medical evacuation is unlikely to be needed. Not appropriate for remote postings or those needing comprehensive travel or maternity coverage.
