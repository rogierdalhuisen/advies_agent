# Globality Yougenio Health Insurance

A comprehensive international health insurance from Globality Health (part of Munich Re), focused on emigrants, retirees, and expats living abroad. Available to virtually all nationalities with no age limit for application, making it notably accessible compared to many alternatives.

Offers four coverage tiers ranging from inpatient-only to full outpatient, pregnancy, and dental coverage. Positioned as a flexible long-term solution for people permanently or semi-permanently settled abroad, rather than those on temporary assignments. Notably, local nationals — people insuring in their own country of residence — cannot be covered.
